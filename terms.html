Fightin' Texas Aggie Terminology

Here are some of the terms traditionally used at Texas A&M.

Aggie a student at Texas A&M who displays true Aggie spirit; also sometimes used to mean any A&M student
Aggieland the Texas A&M campus in College Station
Air Krap a C.T. in an Air Force outfit
artillery beans
baby mustard
bag a bed
Batt The Battalion, the student newspaper
battery acid orange juice
bib a napkin
Black Belt a fish or Pisshead
blankets pancakes
blood ketchup
B.Q. a member of the Fightin' Texas Aggie Band
bullneck meat
cackle eggs
campo a campusology question, about Aggie tradition or history
chow mealtime
cold cow ice cream
cow milk
crapper a restroom or toilet; yelled before a toilet is flushed
Crunchy a C.T. in an Army outfit
crystal a glass or cup
C.T. a member of the Fightin' Texas Aggie Corps of Cadets; also sometimes used to mean a Corps member not in the Band
cush dessert
cut (1) the killing of trees in a forested area for Bonfire; (2) a deficiency found during an inspection
day duck an Aggie living off campus
Dead Zip an Aggie who's finished his senior year
deal bread
dirt pepper
dope coffee
"Drop!" the command to assume the position for push-ups
drop handles to call an upperclassman or thing by its real name
elbows macaroni
elephant (a symbol of) an Aggie senior
fish an Aggie freshman
fizz soft drinks
flush to end a romantic relationship with someone
flush letter a Dear John letter
frat rat a student at Texas A&M in a fraternity
frog (1) an Aggie transfer student; (2) a C.T. who enters the Corps after his fish year
frozen sky ice
gig a minor demerit
GPR grade-point ratio
grease butter
handle a title or alternate name; e.g., "fish" or "Mister"
"Headache!" the yelled warning that a tree has been killed and is about to fall at Bonfire cut
hole a fish's room
horizontal engineering napping
horsefeed corn
"Howdy!" the official greeting of Texas A&M
hump it to assume the position for yelling at maximum volume (bent over at the waist with hands on knees)
hump-it a yell specific to an outfit or dorm
Jarhead a C.T. in a Navy/Marine outfit
Leather Legs an C.T. senior
load the loading of Bonfire logs onto trucks for transport from cut site to stack site
lube gravy
mud chocolate milk
mush oatmeal
non-reg a student at Texas A&M not in the Corps
Ol' Army (1) the way Texas A&M used to be and should always be; (2) a long time ago
ol' lady an Aggie's roommate
piss on an upperclassman's leg to make an excuse to an upperclassman
Pisshead an Aggie sophomore
pitchfork a fork
platter a plate
popeye spinach
pot a helmet used while building Fightin' Texas Aggie Bonfire
Prexy the president of Texas A&M
privilege a prerogative merited by virtue of rank
pull out to assume a privilege not yet earned
Quad the quadrangle where the Corps dorms are located
ram a major demerit
rabbit salad
reg syrup
"Rest!" the command meaning "Be quiet!"
saber a knife
sack out to engage in horizontal engineering
sand salt
sand and salt and pepper
sawdust sugar
s.b. a student at Texas A&M in a sorority
scabs cereal
scope out to look at
Sergebutt an Aggie junior
shingles crackers
shot peas
shotgun Tabasco sauce
shovel a spoon
sky water
sour lemon
spuds potatoes
stack (1) the building of Bonfire on campus; (2) the six-tiered Bonfire structure itself
stud tea
sugar report a letter from a girlfriend or boyfriend
Sully the Lawrence Sullivan Ross statue in front of the Academic Building on campus
sunshine carrots
tadpole a C.T. who enters the Corps halfway through his fish year
teasipper (also: teasip or 'sip) a student at t.u.
treetops broccoli
t.u. that little community college in austin
tub a bowl
12th Man the student body at an Aggie athletic event
two-percenter a student at Texas A&M who does not display true Aggie spirit
wheels doughnuts
whip out to introduce oneself to an upperclassman
White Belt a Sergebutt or Zip
wiggle Jell-O
wildcat (1) a class-specific yell of exhilaration; (2) pineapple
winchester Worcestershire sauce
worms spaghetti
yardbird chicken
yell the equivalent of a cheer at any other school (except much louder); usually followed by a wildcat
Yell Leader one of three Zips and two Sergebutts chosen to lead the 12th Man in yells
Zip an Aggie senior
